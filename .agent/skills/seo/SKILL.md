# Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO is the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results.

## Technical SEO

- **Mobile Friendliness:** Ensure the site is fully responsive.
- **HTTPS:** Ensure the site is served over a secure connection.
- **Crawlability:** Maintain a clean `robots.txt` and an up-to-date XML sitemap.
- **Canonicalization:** Use `rel="canonical"` to prevent duplicate content issues.
- **Structured Data:** Use JSON-LD to provide context to search engines (e.g., Schema.org).

## On-Page SEO

- **Title Tags:** unique, descriptive, and under 60 characters.
- **Meta Descriptions:** Compelling summaries under 160 characters.
- **Heading Hierarchy:** Use only one `<h1>` per page and nested `<h2>`-<h6>` logically.
- **Alt Text:** Use descriptive text for images.
- **Descriptive Links:** Use anchor text that describes the destination (avoid "click here").

## Metrics

- **Organic Traffic:** Visits from search results.
- **Keyword Rankings:** Positions for targeted terms.
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of users who click on your search snippet.
- **Core Web Vitals:** Performance now directly impacts SEO ranking.
